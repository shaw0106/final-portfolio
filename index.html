Hello World!

Shannon believes that curiosity and creativity go hand-in-hand and approaches every situation with an open mind.

Today, Shannon is a recent UX intern and a third year student at Algonquin College studying graphic design. She has been a freelance artist for over ten years and is profecient in several mediums including acrylic, watercolour, charcoal, graphite, Illustrator, InDesign, After Effects and Photoshop.

Shannon is an avid gamer who is all about player-vs-environment gameplay. She is a passionate reader of comic books and is a solid Druid in D&D. Her dream is to find a design family where she can build fluid experiences, illustrate, and create solid projects. Follow, subscribe and enjoy!
